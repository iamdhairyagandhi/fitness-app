# Ultimate Fitness & Nutrition Super-App — Feature Set

## 1. WORKOUT MANAGEMENT

### Core Training
- **Smart Workout Builder** — AI generates personalized programs based on goals, experience, equipment, time availability, and injury history
- **Exercise Library** — 1000+ exercises with 3D animated demos, muscle heat maps, and form cues
- **Real-Time Rep Counter** — Phone camera or wearable tracks reps, tempo, and range of motion using computer vision
- **AI Form Coach** — Live camera feed analyzes form, gives real-time audio/haptic corrections mid-set
- **Auto-Progression Engine** — Tracks your performance curve and auto-adjusts weight/volume/intensity weekly
- **Rest Timer with HRV Recovery** — Suggests optimal rest based on heart rate recovery, not just a fixed clock
- **Superset/Circuit/EMOM/AMRAP Builder** — Supports every training methodology (powerlifting, bodybuilding, CrossFit, calisthenics, Olympic lifting, HIIT, yoga, Pilates, martial arts)
- **Deload Detection** — AI detects fatigue accumulation and auto-programs deload weeks
- **1RM Predictor & Strength Standards** — Estimates maxes from submaximal work, compares to population percentiles
- **Workout Voice Control** — Fully hands-free workout logging via voice commands
- **Gym Equipment Scanner** — Scan a machine's barcode/QR to auto-log the exercise and suggest settings
- **Warm-Up & Cool-Down Generator** — Dynamic warm-ups and mobility routines tailored to the day's workout
- **Training Split Optimizer** — AI suggests optimal split based on recovery data, schedule, and weak points

### Cardio & Conditioning
- **Adaptive Cardio Plans** — Zone 2, HIIT, LISS, Tabata, sprints — auto-programmed based on goals
- **GPS Route Tracking** — Running/cycling with elevation, pace, splits, and route replay
- **Virtual Running Races** — Compete against your past self or friends on the same route
- **VO2 Max Estimation** — Estimated from workout data without lab testing
- **Heart Rate Zone Training** — Real-time zone display with audio coaching

## 2. NUTRITION & DIET

### Food Tracking
- **AI Food Scanner** — Point camera at any plate; AI identifies every item, estimates portions, and logs macros instantly
- **Barcode Scanner** — Scan packaged food, instant nutritional breakdown
- **Receipt/Menu Scanner** — Photograph a restaurant menu or grocery receipt; AI logs everything
- **Natural Language Logging** — Type or say "2 eggs, toast with butter, black coffee" and it's logged perfectly
- **Multi-Database Fusion** — Pulls from USDA, branded food databases, restaurant APIs, and user-submitted entries
- **Micronutrient Dashboard** — Track all 40+ essential vitamins & minerals, not just macros
- **Water & Hydration Tracker** — Smart reminders based on activity level, weather, and body weight
- **Fiber, Sodium, Sugar, Cholesterol Tracking** — With daily targets and warnings
- **Ingredient-Level Breakdown** — See exactly which foods contributed which nutrients
- **Meal Photo Journal** — Visual timeline of everything you've eaten

### Diet Intelligence
- **Adaptive Calorie Targets** — AI adjusts daily calories based on TDEE trends, weight change rate, and energy expenditure
- **Metabolic Adaptation Detection** — Warns when your metabolism is adapting to a deficit and suggests diet breaks
- **Macro Cycling** — Auto-programs carb cycling, calorie cycling, and refeed days
- **Diet Phase Management** — Bulk → Cut → Maintenance transitions with smart reverse dieting protocols
- **Flexible Dieting / IIFYM Mode** — Remaining macro budget display with food suggestions to hit targets
- **Diet Templates** — Keto, Paleo, Vegan, Mediterranean, Carnivore, Intermittent Fasting, DASH, Whole30, etc.
- **Allergy & Intolerance Profiles** — Flags foods containing allergens, auto-filters recipes
- **Fasting Tracker** — Tracks fasting windows, auto-adjusts feeding window calories
- **Gut Health Score** — Tracks fiber diversity, fermented food intake, and prebiotic/probiotic balance

## 3. MEAL PLANNING & RECIPES

- **AI Meal Plan Generator** — Creates full weekly meal plans hitting exact macro/micro targets, respecting preferences, budget, and prep time
- **Recipe Database** — Thousands of recipes with macro breakdowns, scalable servings
- **Smart Grocery List** — Auto-generated from meal plan, organized by store aisle, with cost estimates
- **Grocery Store Integration** — One-tap ordering from Instacart, Amazon Fresh, Walmart, etc.
- **Meal Prep Mode** — Batch cooking plans with container packing guides
- **Leftover Optimizer** — Suggests recipes using ingredients you already have (fridge scan via camera)
- **Restaurant Menu Advisor** — Before dining out, paste/scan a menu; AI suggests best options for your goals
- **Recipe Macro Adjuster** — Modify any recipe to hit specific macro targets (swap ingredients, adjust portions)
- **Cultural Cuisine Support** — Accurate nutritional data for cuisines worldwide, not just Western food
- **Family/Household Mode** — Plan meals for the whole family with individual macro targets

## 4. BODY COMPOSITION & BIOMETRICS

- **Weight Trend Analytics** — Moving averages, rate of change, projected timeline to goal weight
- **Progress Photo AI** — Take standardized photos; AI analyzes body fat %, muscle distribution, and visual changes over time
- **Body Measurement Tracker** — Waist, hips, chest, arms, thighs, calves — with trend charts
- **DEXA/InBody Import** — Import professional scan data for calibration
- **3D Body Scan** — Phone LiDAR creates 3D body model, tracks volume changes over time
- **Body Fat Estimation** — Multi-method: AI photo analysis, Navy method, skinfold calculator
- **Muscle Balance Analysis** — Identifies asymmetries and lagging body parts from photos and lift data
- **Posture Analysis** — Camera-based posture assessment with corrective exercise recommendations

- **AI Personal Trainer Chat** — Conversational AI that knows your entire history, answers any fitness/nutrition question contextually

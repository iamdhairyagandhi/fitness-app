Help & FAQ

Quick answers for account setup, workout tracking, nutrition targets, Apple Health, data controls, and app review support.

How do I change units, theme, or reminders?

Open BodyPilot, go to Profile, then App Settings. You can adjust units, rest timer, theme, color scheme, and notification preferences there.

Where do I change calorie or macro targets?

Open Profile, then Customize Macros. You can update calories, protein, carbs, fat, and water targets without changing your diet template.

How do I use Apple Health activity?

Open Profile, then Wearable Devices. Enable Apple Health permissions so BodyPilot can use workout activity and calorie burn where supported.

How do I delete my account?

Open Profile, Account Details, then Delete Account. This starts permanent deletion for your BodyPilot account data.

How do I report content or block someone?

In Community, use the menu on a feed post to report, hide, or block. User search cards also include a block control.

How do I export my data?

Open Profile, App Settings, then Data & Privacy. BodyPilot can export available workout and measurement records.

Still need help?

Send a note with your device, app version, and what you were trying to do. Screenshots or screen recordings help a lot.

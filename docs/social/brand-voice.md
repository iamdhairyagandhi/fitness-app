# BodyPilot Brand Voice

## Positioning

BodyPilot is the AI fitness companion that helps users train smarter, eat better, recover well, and understand progress in one place.

## Voice

- Clear.
- Energetic.
- Practical.
- Trustworthy.
- Slightly futuristic, but grounded.

## Say

- "AI fitness companion"
- "Workout, nutrition, progress, and recovery tracking"
- "Log faster"
- "Review before saving"
- "Train smarter"
- "See progress"
- "Orbit"

## Avoid

- Guaranteed results.
- Medical advice.
- Shame-based fitness language.
- Overpromising AI.
- "Hack your body" style claims.
- Before/after transformation claims unless they are real, consented, and contextualized.

## Default CTA

```text
Follow for the build and launch.
```

## App Store CTA

```text
Download BodyPilot on the App Store.
```
